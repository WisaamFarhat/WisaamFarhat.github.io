ABOUT ME

Artist. Freerunner. Engineer

In an age where the information superhighway, social media, and the countless mediums for unrestricted entertainment are at our literal fingertips all hours of the day, one would think that as inhabitants of modern cities we should be happier than ever. Unfortunately, that doesn't seem to be the case at all. Depression, identity crises, and anxiety disorders are but a few of the plethora of issues afflicting individuals of all ages, and they seem to be on the rise too. When does it end? When you take control. When you take responsibility for your actions, your life, and extract the essentials from the detritus. Find a creative outlet, focus on the things YOU enjoy, develop positive habits and skills, and work hard to reach your goals. Per aspera ad astra.

Wisaam O Farhat





Welcome to my website. I found my creative outlets back in 2009-2010, when I was still in middle school. My love of certain video games and their iconic characters and worlds sparked a desire in me to draw, and though the passion for the protagonists waned as I aged, the artistic skills I developed remained and flourished. Around the same time I discovered my interest in two other pastimes, freerunning and chess, both skills which I have also developed with time and am proud of. Professionally I got a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the American University of Sharjah, and graduated with a Magna Cum Laude. Have a look at my works below; note that earlier attempts are (typically) closer to the top of a category. Be critical, and enjoy :).
